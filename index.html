IBM MSP Meet Up

Meet Up on Mainframe Modernisation Summit

19 Mar 2024 @ Hilton Bangalore Embassy GolfLinks

Managed Service Providers (MSPs) should consider Mainframe Modernisation for several strategic investment that can lead to improved efficiency, scalability, cost savings, security, compliance, user experience, innovation, and competitiveness. It helps organisations stay relevant, agile, and adaptable in today's rapidly evolving technological landscape to modernising existing mainframe infrastructure & applications while preserving critical business logic, minimising disruption, and mitigating risks associated with migration to entirely new platforms.

In an era defined by digital transformation, it's imperative to explore how Mainframe technology is not just adapting but thriving through AI, AIOPS, Hypersalers and Data Modernisation while IBM's Watson Code Assistant for Z is revolutionising the Modernisation strategies.
