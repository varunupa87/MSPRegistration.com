IBM MSP Meet Up

Meet Up on Mainframe Modernisation Summit

19 Mar 2024 @ Hilton Bangalore Embassy GolfLinks

Managed Service Providers (MSPs) should consider in-place mainframe modernization for several strategic investment that can lead to improved efficiency, scalability, cost savings, security, compliance, user experience, innovation, and competitiveness. It helps organizations stay relevant, agile, and adaptable in today's rapidly evolving technological landscape to modernizing existing mainframe infrastructure while preserving critical business logic, minimizing disruption, and mitigating risks associated with migration to entirely new platforms.

In an era defined by digital transformation, it's imperative to explore how Mainframe technology is not just adapting but thriving through AI, AIOPS, Hypersalers and Data Modernisation.

IBM MSP Meet Up is exclusively designed to focused on the cutting-edge convergence of Mainframe Modernisation.
